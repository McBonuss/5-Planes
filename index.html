Your Ultimate Hub for Paper Airplane Enthusiasts

Welcome to 5 Planes, the go-to destination for paper airplane lovers of all ages! Whether you're a beginner learning your first folds, an expert pushing the limits of design, or a competitor aiming for the longest flight, we’re here to connect you with the world of paper aviation.

Our platform is dedicated to fostering a community of enthusiasts who share a passion for creativity, engineering, and the joy of flight. From tutorials on crafting the perfect paper airplane to tips on achieving record-breaking distances, we provide resources for everyone, regardless of skill level.

Explore our collection of designs, participate in exciting events, and connect with like-minded individuals who share your enthusiasm for paper aviation. Whether you're looking to improve your folding techniques, learn about the science of aerodynamics, or simply have fun, 5 Planes is the perfect place to start your journey.

Discover Exciting Paper Airplane Events

Join the world of paper aviation through thrilling events and competitions! Whether you're looking to showcase your folding skills, compete for the longest flight, or simply connect with fellow enthusiasts, our events are designed for everyone.

From local meetups to international championships, our events bring together paper airplane enthusiasts from all walks of life. Experience the joy of friendly competition, learn new techniques from experts, and share your passion with a vibrant community.

Our events are not just about competition—they're about celebrating creativity, innovation, and the love of flight. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or a curious beginner, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Don't miss the chance to be part of these unforgettable experiences!
