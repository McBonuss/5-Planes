Paper flight more than simple folds


Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. It is a form of art that has been practiced for centuries.


Where it all began

Chinese engineers are thought to have invented what could be considered the earliest paper planes around 2,000 years ago. But these ancient gliders, usually crafted from bamboo and paper or linen, resembled kites more than the dart-shaped fliers that have earned numerous Guinness World Records in recent years -Popular Mechancs
