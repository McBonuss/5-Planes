Discover Exciting Paper Airplane Events

Join the world of paper aviation through thrilling events and competitions! Whether you're looking to showcase your folding skills, compete for the longest flight, or simply connect with fellow enthusiasts, our events are designed for everyone. From local meetups to international challenges, there's always something happening in the world of paper airplanes.

Stay updated on upcoming events, learn about record-breaking attempts, and find opportunities to participate in workshops and exhibitions. Take your passion for paper airplanes to the next level and be part of a vibrant community that celebrates creativity and innovation in flight!


Upcoming Events

International Paper Plane Day

Date: 26th March 2025

Location: Worldwide

Details: Celebrate the art of paper folding on International Paper Plane Day! Join enthusiasts from around the globe in a day of fun, creativity, and friendly competition. Share your designs, test your flights, and connect with fellow flyers in this annual celebration of paper aviation.

Flight School Workshop

Date: 10th April 2025

Location: New York, USA

Details: Learn the art of paper airplane folding from the experts! Join our Flight School Workshop for a hands-on experience in design, construction, and flight techniques. Discover the science behind paper airplanes, explore different models, and take your skills to new heights in this interactive session.

World Record Challenge

Date: 15th May 2025

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Details: Compete for the title of World Record Holder in the ultimate paper airplane challenge! Test your design, launch, and flight skills against the best in the world as you aim for the longest flight distance. Join us for an exciting day of competition, camaraderie, and high-flying action!

Flight Fest Carnival

Date: 20th June 2025

Location: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Details: Experience the thrill of flight at Flight Fest Carnival! Join us for a day of games, contests, and entertainment as we celebrate the joy of paper airplanes. Participate in competitions, watch aerial displays, and enjoy a festive atmosphere of creativity and fun. Don't miss this exciting event for flyers of all ages!
